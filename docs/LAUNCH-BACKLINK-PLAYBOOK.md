# Citensity — Launch & Backlink Playbook

**Why this doc:** citensity.com is a brand-new domain with ~zero backlinks. On-page SEO/GEO is
already strong (205 articles, schema, sitemap, GSC, IndexNow). The #1 remaining ranking lever is
**authority** — real links and mentions — and that's operator work, not code. This is the ordered,
paste-ready plan. Expect meaningful Google rankings in **2–6 months**; AI-engine citations (GEO)
can come faster.

## Weeks 1–4 — The roundup play (highest-value links in this niche)

Every competitor publishes "best GEO/AEO tools" listicles — those pages rank, and they add new
tools. Getting listed = a relevant, high-intent backlink + referral traffic.

**Targets (search these, email the authors):**
"best GEO tools 2026" · "best AEO tools" · "AI visibility tools" · "generative engine optimization
software" — e.g. the roundups by Conductor, NoGood, SitePoint, Surmado, Conbersa, and niche
newsletters that cover AI search.

**Outreach template (short, honest, no begging):**
> Subject: Addition for your GEO tools roundup
>
> Hi {name} — I read your "{article title}" piece. We just launched **Citensity**
> (citensity.com): unlike monitoring-only tools it closes the full loop — tracks AI visibility,
> generates answer-first pages grounded in the brand's real facts, and **publishes them to
> WordPress/Webflow/Shopify**. That publish step is the gap most tools on your list leave open.
>
> If it fits the roundup, happy to provide a demo login or screenshots. Either way, thanks for
> the piece — it's one of the better ones on the topic.
>
> — Abhijay, founder

Track sends in a simple sheet; follow up once after ~5 days; stop after one follow-up.

## Launch (when ready — one focused day)

- [ ] **Product Hunt launch** — ship day checklist: gallery images from the live site, first
      comment from the founder explaining the "full-loop" wedge, reply to every comment same-day.
- [ ] **Founder LinkedIn post** — the honest build story ("we rebranded to Citensity, here's why
      names matter for AI citation") outperforms product pitches.
- [ ] **Hacker News "Show HN"** — only with an honest technical angle (e.g. how the citability
      scorer works). HN punishes marketing speak.
- [ ] Cross-post the launch to relevant subreddits (r/SEO, r/bigseo — read each sub's self-promo
      rules first) and to Indie Hackers.

## Ongoing (weekly habit, ~1 hr/week)

- [ ] **HARO / journalist requests** (Connectively, Qwoted, Help a B2B Writer): answer 2–3
      AI-search/SEO queries a week as the founder — quotes become DR60+ links.
- [ ] **Guest posts / podcasts:** pitch the GEO angle ("how to get cited by ChatGPT") to marketing
      blogs and podcasts — the topic is hot and underserved by practitioners.
- [ ] **Keep publishing** long-tail resources (already happening — 205 and counting) and interlink
      new articles via `related[]`.
- [ ] **Monitor:** GSC Performance (rankings) weekly + GA4 (traffic). When a page gets impressions
      but a low position, strengthen it (more depth, more internal links pointing at it).

---

## What NOT to do

- **No bought links / PBNs / link farms** — a new domain is exactly what Google's spam systems
  watch; one penalty erases everything.
- **No mass directory spam** beyond the curated list above — 20 good directories beat 500 junk ones.
- **No fabricated reviews/testimonials** — consistent with the product's own honesty positioning.
